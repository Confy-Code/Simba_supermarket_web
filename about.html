Our Story

Founded in the heart of Kigali, Simba Supermarket has grown from a local grocery store to Rwanda's premier online shopping destination. Our mission is to provide fresh, high-quality products to every household in Rwanda with the convenience of modern technology.

Community First

We source our fresh produce directly from local Rwandan farmers, supporting our community and ensuring the shortest path from farm to your table.

Quality Assured

Every item on our shelves and in our warehouse undergoes rigorous quality checks to ensure you receive only the best.

Innovation

By integrating modern e-commerce solutions, we're making shopping easier, faster, and more accessible for everyone.
